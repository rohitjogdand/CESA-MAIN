ELEVATOR SAGA

ALL ABOUT THE EVENT

"Elevator Saga" is an interactive programming game that simulates the challenges of efficiently managing elevator systems in multi-floor buildings. Players are tasked with writing algorithms to control elevators, optimizing passenger transportation while minimizing wait times and energy consumption. This educational tool offers hands-on experience in algorithmic thinking, data structures, concurrency, and optimization strategies. Used in events like coding competitions, Elevator Saga is a practical and engaging platform for students and programmers to hone their skills in real-time systems and algorithm design.
